# Outreach Engine

**A drafting assistant for outreach to your existing contacts — past customers, opted-in subscribers, warm referrals. Not cold lists.**

> **Read this first.** The Outreach Engine is only built for clients who can demonstrate consent or permission to contact the recipients. Cold lists, scraped contacts, purchased data, or contacts harvested from restricted platforms are out of scope. This is non-negotiable and tied to the AI, Data and Security Addendum §5 and §7.

## The work it retires

The hours a small business owner spends writing personalised re-engagement, follow-up, win-back, upsell, event-invite or newsletter messages to people who already know them.

## Inputs

- Your **permissioned** contact list (CRM exports, past customer database, opted-in newsletter list, event attendees who consented to follow-up).
- The thing you want to say (re-engagement, new product, event, check-in, ask for feedback, ask for a referral).
- Your tone, brand voice, signoff and any examples of past messages that worked.

## Outputs

- Personalised draft messages per recipient, with the relevant context pulled in.
- A sending schedule with throttling (no batch-blast).
- A reply-triage hookup so responses route to a human, not back into the bot.

## Typical integrations

HubSpot, Pipedrive, Attio, Mailchimp, Klaviyo, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, Customer.io, Postmark, Resend, Gmail (with proper SPF/DKIM), Outlook, SMS providers (Twilio, MessageBird) where consent permits.

## What's included

- Contact list intake and de-duplication.
- Consent and unsubscribe-list verification (you provide the proof; we wire it in).
- Personalised draft generation per recipient.
- Throttled sending schedule with unsubscribe handling and identification (per ACMA spam rules).
- Reply-triage routing.
- Setup notes, compliance checklist, handover.

## What's not included

- **Cold outreach.** If you can't demonstrate consent or a pre-existing business relationship, Engine Labs will decline the build. This is per Addendum §5 and §7.
- **Scraping.** No contact data is sourced from LinkedIn, scraped websites, restricted platforms, purchased lists or aggregator tools where the source breaches platform terms.
- Bulk SMS or robocalls.
- Replacement of your email or CRM platform.
- Outcome guarantees on open rate, reply rate, revenue or list growth.

## Human review

Sending is throttled and can be paused at any time. The first batch in any campaign is reviewed manually before the rest sends. Replies go to a human, never back into the model. Unsubscribes are honoured immediately and the suppression list is checked before every send.
