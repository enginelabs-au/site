# Insight Engine

**A dashboard and weekly written summary that turns your raw data into the meeting you'd otherwise have to prepare for.**

## The work it retires

The Monday-morning analyst job: pulling numbers from Stripe, GA, your CRM and a spreadsheet, putting them in a deck, writing the commentary, and explaining what changed.

## Inputs

- Your existing data sources (Stripe, Google Analytics, HubSpot, Shopify, Airtable, Sheets, a CSV export, a Postgres database).
- The questions you actually care about each week — not "all the metrics", just the ones that drive decisions.
- The audience (you, your team, your board, your investors).

## Outputs

- A live dashboard with the metrics that matter and the filters you need.
- A scheduled written summary (weekly or monthly) in plain English, sent where your team reads.
- Anomaly alerts when a metric moves outside its normal range.

## Typical integrations

Stripe, Google Analytics 4, HubSpot, Shopify, Klaviyo, Mailchimp, Airtable, Google Sheets, Notion, Postgres/Supabase, BigQuery, Metabase, Lightdash, Looker Studio, Plausible, Fathom.

## What's included

- Up to 4 connected data sources.
- A dashboard (built in your tool of choice — we don't lock you into ours).
- A scheduled AI-written summary with commentary.
- Up to 3 anomaly alert rules.
- Documentation of every metric definition (no "what does this number actually mean" arguments).
- Handover.

## What's not included

- Data warehouse design or large-scale extract, transform, and load.
- Custom statistical modelling, forecasting or attribution beyond off-the-shelf approaches.
- Replacement of your analytics or BI tooling.
- Investor-grade financial modelling or auditing.

## Human review

The Insight Engine summarises and alerts. It does not make business decisions. Every summary is clearly labelled as AI-generated commentary on the underlying numbers — the numbers are the source of truth, the commentary is the read.
